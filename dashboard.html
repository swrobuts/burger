Pricing Power Analysis: AOV grew 55% over 9 years (€14.50 → €22.48), far outpacing unit growth (84% order growth). This demonstrates exceptional pricing power. 2023 was peak (+14.0%) — likely aggressive inflation response. Deceleration 2024-2026 (+2.5-4.9% annually) suggests price sensitivity emerging. Gross margin pressure: While AOV surged, order growth slowed, indicating some volume trade-off. Premium burger adoption (Beyond, Truffle) enabled AOV expansion without commoditization risk. Caution: Further increases may hit elasticity ceiling; focus on volume recovery in 2026+.
